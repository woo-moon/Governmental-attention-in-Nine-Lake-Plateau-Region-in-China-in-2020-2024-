# GovEco
Original Data for research article: Unpacking the Problem- and Politically Driven Orientations in Governmental Decision-Making in Aquatic Ecology-Centered Watershed Governance: A Case Study of Nine-Plateau-Lake Region in China
Files in the folder and data source:
gpr_yunnan: policy texts released by People's Government of Yunnan, collected from https://www.yn.gov.cn/zwgk/zfgb/
gpr_dali:  policy texts released by People's Government of Dali, collected from https://www.dali.gov.cn/dlrmzf/zfgb/zfgb.shtml
gpr_honghe: policy texts released by People's Government of Honghe, collected from https://www.hh.gov.cn/zfxxgk/hhzrmzfgb/a2024n/dsrq.htm
gpr_kunming: policy texts released by People's Government of Kunming, collected from https://www.km.gov.cn/zfgbhz/
gpr_lijiang: policy texts released by People's Government of Lijiang, collected from https://www.lijiang.gov.cn/ljsrmzf/zfgb/zfgb.shtml
gpr_yuxi: policy texts released by People's Government of Yuxi, collected from https://www.yuxi.gov.cn/yxszfxxgk/tz/20240913/1555275.html
